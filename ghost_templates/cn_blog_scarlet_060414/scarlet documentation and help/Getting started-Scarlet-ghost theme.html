Before you start

some links may be useful to get started:
http://docs.ghost.org/
https://en.ghost.org/forum/

Instalation:

  • Open to your FTP client (or explore your ghost directory if you are hosted ghost locally).
  • Navigate to ghost/content/themes.
  • Paste main scarlet folder.
  • Restart ghost and log in to your dashboard.
  • In settings under themes select scarlet from dropdown menu. and click save.

That's the end of the installation process. now it's time to configure your theme.

Configuration

For a full functionality of this theme, please follow the below steps.

General Configuration

  • Log in to your dashboard and open settings page of your blog.
  • There fill inputs like blog name, description etc.
  • On blog logo you can add your own logo, it will be shown on your blog.
  • On blog cover you can add a cover, it will be shown on your blog header. other wise if you leave blank the header will remain flat color.
  • In user section, fill areas and add your avatar. That info's and your avatar will be shown at the end of every single post you write.
  • Make sure your avater is square ie. width and height are same. recommended size of avater is 200px X 200px .
